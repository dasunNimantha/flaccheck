flaccheck

Verify your FLACs are truly lossless — not MP3/AAC in disguise

How it works

flaccheck analyzes audio fingerprints to detect transcoded files sold as lossless.

  • Fast — spectral cutoff + hi-res checks (~seconds per file)
  • Balanced — adds quantization + artifacts (recommended)
  • Max — exhaustive search, slowest but most thorough
GENUINE No lossy fingerprint SUSPICIOUS Some indicators TRANSCODED Strong lossy signal INCONCLUSIVE Too band-limited
